--- layout: post title: Podcasts and mobile device quick view categories: [] tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- One thing that I continually find to be a problem is in deciding which of my podcasts to listen to on a mobile device (iPod or iPhone) that has a limited amount of screen space. Very often the podcast entry title will not contain anything about that specific episode. The result is that I have to start listening to it to know what it is about. Having an episode title that contains a least a little about the content would be very helpful.
