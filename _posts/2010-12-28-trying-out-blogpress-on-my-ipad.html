--- layout: post title: Trying out BlogPress on my iPad categories: [] tags: - BlogPress - ipad status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- I have been using a browser to post to my blog and decided to try using the BlogPress app on my iPad. One tidbit on it, you have to enable the XML-rpc remote publishing protocol to do this (found in site admin/settings/writing). The main reason that I am trying this is I am finding that I spend more and more time using my ipad and less on a laptop/computer. I still do not have the hang of rapid typing on the iPad however so I make many more mistakes. - Posted using BlogPress from my iPad
