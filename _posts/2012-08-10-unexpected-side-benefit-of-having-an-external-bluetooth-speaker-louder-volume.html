--- layout: post title: Unexpected side benefit of having an external Bluetooth speaker (louder volume) categories: [] tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- I recently bought a Beacon Audio Phoenix external Bluetooth speaker which I have been using for listening to podcasts on my iPhone. I just had another benefit from it today while listening to a webinar ("Safety Critical Software Development with Rhapsody" by Bruce Douglass at IBM) on my MacBook Air. I had the volume all the way up but because of a fan in my office I could not hear it very well. I paired the Beacon with my Air and was able to increase the volume to a much easier to listen to level. I've been very happy with the Beacon so far and recommend it for value and flexibility.
