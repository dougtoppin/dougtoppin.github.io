--- layout: post title: Apple Time Capsule Guest Network Performance categories: [] tags: - apple - mobile devices status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- I use an Apple Time Capsule as my wireless router and it has worked very well since setting it up a few months ago. I recently enabled a guest network on it while a friend was visiting and have noticed a distinct reduction in network throughput. I did a little checking and Apple says that their may be a 20% drop in performance. It is clear that you should only enable the guest net when you actually need it. - Posted using BlogPress from my iPad
